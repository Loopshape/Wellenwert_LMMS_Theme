WELLENWERT – LMMS THEME
-----------------------

I've taken some of the dark-themes from the LMMS-website and changed some color informations of the theme-palette.
Just copy the content of the inner ZIP-archiv into the default-folder within "/usr/share/lmms/themes". 
Restart LMMS, there'ya go!!!



>>> Wellenwert – LMMS Theme <<< 

This is a theme for LMMS (Linux-Multimedia-Studio), that was taken and modified from me {Loopshape aka.Wellenwert} 
from the LMMS.io website... 

I modified icons and created a colorful palette with a wide spectrum of all ranged color-values!!! 
This theme is based originally on another "dark" themes, that were put together and recolored.
